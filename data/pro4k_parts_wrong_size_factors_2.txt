(For dies only) Which CAD design software do you use? When you design the dies, do you add a clearance (horizontal shaft gap) or a cement gap to your models? For example when using Exocad, a clearance of 20 microns would be necessary. If using 3Shape, then a cement gap of 30 microns (0.030mm) would be necessary.
