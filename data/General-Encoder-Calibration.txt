Category is General. How to calibrate Encoder.
1. remove the basin of the printer, then go to Maintenance > Service > Position Encoders
2. Calibrate Position Encoders and enter the mass of your calibration weight. 
3. Place the weight on each of the encoders in turn, as the process instructs. 
4. Zero on the encoders and check each one individually so that the blue dot moves to the correct corner when you apply pressure. 
5. Zero in again and check for stability. 
6. After replacing the basin, zero again.
