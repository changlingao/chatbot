Composer Multistack
To use the multistack feature:
1. open a second build tab for the upper layer, and put parts and supports in there. 
2. For the lower layer, you can click multi stack. 
3. Click apply under the scaffold plate, and the baseplate should appear. 
4. Then under scaffold columns, click add. You can then left click on the plate to add the columns.
5. When you print, there is a multi stack list. To select the different build tabs as layers and the order they print in.
If you go to the help feature (F1), please see chapter seven in the manual for a detailed explanation of this. There is also a video on youtube explaining the process : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aW9epdEawrA
