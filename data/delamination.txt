What is delamination?
Delamination, commonly referred to as layer separation, is a print defect that occurs when two or more layers have not bonded together properly. Here are two examples of delamination:
- https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/delaminating2.jpg
- https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/delaminating.jpg

Known causes and their solutions are:
1. Insufficient Supports - The number one cause of delamination is incorrect placement of supports.
2. Low material level - If the material level is too low, you may see delamination.
