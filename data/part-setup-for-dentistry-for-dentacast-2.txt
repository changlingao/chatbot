Part setup for dentistry for DentaCAST recommendations
Crown and bridge metal prosthetics are recommended to be built with a 50 µm layer thickness. The occlusal surface should be facing the build platform. Patient interfacing areas should face away from the build platform.
Crown and mridge lithium disilicate prosthetics are recommended to be built with a 50 µm layer thickness. The occlusal surface should be facing the build platform. Patient interfacing areas should face away from the build platform.
Metal partial frameworks are recommended to be built with a 75 µm layer thickness. The intaglio surface should be facing away from the build platform.
