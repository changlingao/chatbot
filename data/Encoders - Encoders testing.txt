Category is Encoders. Troubleshoot encoders
1. Check whether the encoders are all responding on the printer. 
2. Then Remove the tray and platform from the printer.
3. Go to Maintenance> Position Encoders> Zero Position Encoders. 
4. Zero the encoders and wait for the blue dot to return to the centre. 
5. Press on each corner of the basin in turn, and the blue dot will move to the appropriate corner. 
6. Zero again.
