Category is Composer. Clearing composer settings for Windows.
It is important to note that reinstalling composer will not remove any settings for composer.To clear composer settings, please follow the steps below:
1. Browse to Program Files/Asiga/Composer
2. Press Shift and right click. Select open Powershell window here
3. Run Composer.exe --profile-reset
After clearing the settings, please uninstall then redownload and reinstall Composer from here:
https://www.asiga.com/accounts/software/
This will provide you with a fresh installation of Composer.
