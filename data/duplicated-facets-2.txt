Duplicated facets STL file error
If your error message is suffixed by '.stl', you are experiencing an STL error. This means there are duplicated facets in the model itself & can only be rectified within a CAD program. Typical names for this repair function are:
- Mesh repair
- Mesh heal
- Boolean repair
