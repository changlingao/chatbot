Please take a torch light or the light from your smartphone and shine it onto the glass from different angles to detect any smudges, there should be no streaks, resin residue or smears, fingerprints, dust or any other smudges. You can clean the glass with IPA alcohol and a soft, lint free cloth or optical cloth and give it a final wipe with window cleaner. Afterwards, check the glass with the torch light again.
