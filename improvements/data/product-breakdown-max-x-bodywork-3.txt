Product breakdown of MAX X bodywork. The Connectivity Panel
The rear panel is constructed of a curved black metal sheet with cut-outs housing the power and network plugs. This panel can be quickly removed for service and access to all printer electronics. The stainless steel panel on the lower half is the bottom rear cover. (https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/maxx-rear-nobg.jpg)
