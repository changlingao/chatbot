Category is Resins. Fusion Gray. 
The FusionGray can solidify and crystallise, especially when the environment is a bit cooler, or for example during air transport. In this case, it is required to warm up the resin, please see the Instructions for Use (IFU) from the Asiga account: https://www.asiga.com/accounts/resources/Materials/Asiga/IFU%20%28Instructions%20for%20Use%29/Asiga%20FusionGRAY%20IFU.pdf
The guide mentions 3 possibilities to heat up the resin.
