Part setup for dentistry for DentaBASE general information
High impact resistance, ease of finishing and high stain resistance makes Asiga DentaBASE a robust solution for manufacturing denture bases. Asiga DentaBASE serves in producing the base to your two-part denture by combining with 3D printed denture teeth, milled denture teeth, or carded denture teeth. 
For a patient compliant and comfortable digital denture, it all starts with Asiga DentaBASE.
