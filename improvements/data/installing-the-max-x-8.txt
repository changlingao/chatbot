Installing the MAX X or MAX. Connecting to your MAX X or MAX on a wireless direct connection.
Wireless Direct allows the printer to act as a wireless access point. This can be used if there is no router available.
To connect to your printer on a wireless direct connection, please perform the following steps:
1. From the Main Menu select Settings. (https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/Main-Menu.jpg)
2. Select Wireless Direct. Note thatÂ this will prevent any WiFi connections. (https://support.asiga.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/Settings-WirelessDirect-1.jpg)

The default settings for Wireless Direct are:
- Wireless Network: Printer name
- IP address: 172.27.10.1
- Subnet Mask: 255.255.255.0
