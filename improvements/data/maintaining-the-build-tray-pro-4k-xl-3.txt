PRO 4K and PRO 4K XL Disposing of or replacing the build tray
When the build tray has reached 100%, it must be replaced. Old build trays contain uncured resin that must be dealt with according to local laws. Please place the build tray in direct sunlight or a UV chamber until all the remaining resin has cured.Â 
For instructions on how to change the build tray, refer to the following video: https://player.vimeo.com/video/740584988
**Uncured resin is classified as hazardous to aquatic life.**
**Eye or skin contact with uncured resin may cause irritation. Always wear nitrile gloves.**
