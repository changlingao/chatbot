Cleaning MAX X glass surfaces
Keeping the basin glass clean on your MAX X is extremely important. The projector will reflect the image through this window and smudges will impact its performance. Please refer to the following video for instructions on how to clean the basin glass: https://player.vimeo.com/video/736386770

**Eye or skin contact with uncured resin may cause irritation**
**Always wear nitrile gloves**
**IPA is a flammable chemical**
