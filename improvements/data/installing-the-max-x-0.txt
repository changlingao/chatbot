Installing the MAX X or MAX
To install the MAX X or MAX, please perform the following steps:
1. Connect to a power source
2. Select a language for the printer
3. Register the printer on your Asiga account
4. Connect to your MAX X printer through either a wired/ethernet connection, wifi or a wireless connection
5. Calibrate the build platform
6. Validate the build platform
7. Insert the build tray and resin
