MAX Audio basins
The audiology basin is not available in the online shop, but can be mentioned when placing an order. In this case, just enter the inquiry into the notes that you would like to order X amount of audiology basins. The Asiga part number for that would be PN/03707.
You would require the audiology build trays for the audiology basin. The description would be: 
1. 1L MAX Build Tray - Audiology, Product code: PN/03758, with dealer price of $51 USD
2. 2L MAX Build Tray - Audiology, Product code: PN/03708, with dealer price of $78 USD
Reseller prices for the audio-basin would be $840USD.
